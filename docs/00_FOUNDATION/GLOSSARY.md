# GLOSSARY.md

## Purpose

This glossary defines the core language of aiio, the Organizational Intelligence System and the category of Self-Improving Organizations.

The goal is to create a shared vocabulary for product, website, sales, investors and future documentation.

---

## Self-Improving Organization

A Self-Improving Organization is an organization that can continuously preserve its knowledge, understand how it works and improve its structures, processes and decisions over time.

It is not only automated.

It is capable of learning.

---

## Organizational Resilience

Organizational Resilience is the ability of an organization to remain capable of action despite disruption, demographic change, knowledge loss, complexity and volatility.

Resilience requires more than documentation.

It requires organizational memory, context and understanding.

---

## Organizational Knowledge

Organizational Knowledge is the collective know-how of an organization.

It includes processes, responsibilities, structures, documents, systems, decisions, experience, exceptions, routines and informal knowledge.

---

## Organizational Memory

Organizational Memory is the preserved and accessible representation of what an organization knows.

It prevents knowledge from disappearing when people leave, systems change or processes evolve.

---

## Organizational Context

Organizational Context is the situational understanding required to interpret knowledge correctly.

It explains how processes, roles, systems, rules, goals and responsibilities relate to each other.

Without organizational context, AI cannot reliably support enterprise work.

---

## Organizational Understanding

Organizational Understanding is the ability to interpret how an organization actually works.

It connects knowledge, context, processes, responsibilities and systems into a coherent model of the organization.

---

## Organizational Intelligence

Organizational Intelligence is the capability of an organization to continuously capture, understand and activate its collective knowledge.

It is the foundation for resilient and self-improving organizations.

---

## Organizational Intelligence System

An Organizational Intelligence System, short OIS, is the software infrastructure that creates, maintains and activates organizational intelligence.

It connects organizational knowledge with people, AI agents and enterprise systems.

The OIS is not another business application.

It is the operating system for organizational knowledge.

---

## Grounding Layer

The Grounding Layer captures and structures organizational knowledge.

It creates the reliable knowledge foundation required for organizational intelligence.

In the aiio platform, this role is represented by ProcessCollector®.

---

## Understanding Layer

The Understanding Layer interprets organizational knowledge.

It identifies relationships, patterns, processes, risks, gaps and improvement opportunities.

In the aiio platform, this role is represented by ProcessMagnet®.

---

## Activation Layer

The Activation Layer makes organizational knowledge usable in work.

It enables AI agents, chats, APIs, enterprise applications and people to act from a shared organizational understanding.

In the aiio platform, this role is represented by ProcessForge®.

---

## DataForge

DataForge is the future extension of the OIS that connects organizational intelligence with real operational data from systems such as ERP, CRM and other enterprise applications.

Its purpose is to enrich organizational understanding with live business reality.

---

## Organizational Memory Crisis

The Organizational Memory Crisis describes the growing risk that organizations lose critical knowledge faster than they can preserve and reuse it.

It is driven by demographic change, employee turnover, restructuring, system fragmentation, increasing complexity and volatile markets.

---

## AI Alignment Layer

The AI Alignment Layer ensures that AI agents operate within the organizational context, rules, responsibilities, goals and constraints of a specific enterprise.

It prevents AI from acting on generic knowledge alone.

---

## Operating System for the Organization

The Operating System for the Organization is the conceptual role of the OIS.

Just as a computer operating system provides shared services and context for applications, the OIS provides shared organizational memory, understanding and context for people, AI agents and enterprise systems.

---

---

## Terms to Avoid or Use Carefully

* BPM software
  Use only when contrasting with traditional process management.

* AI chatbot
  Too narrow. aiio is not a chatbot.

* Process documentation tool
  Too narrow. ProcessCollector is the Grounding Layer of the OIS.

* Automation platform
  Too narrow. Automation is an outcome, not the category.

* DAO
  Avoid on public website unless explained carefully. It may create blockchain associations.
