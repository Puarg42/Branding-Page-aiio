# 08 - Redundancy Before / After

## Purpose

Document the major repeated statements reduced during Sprint 008.

| Original repeated statement | Action taken | Final owner section |
| --- | --- | --- |
| "The future belongs to organizations that understand themselves." appeared as both opening and closing logic. | Kept as the Hero-owned category opening; Footer was shortened to a simpler action-oriented close. | Hero |
| "Organizations understand themselves" appeared across Hero, Missing Layer, New Organization and Company. | Reduced later repetitions and replaced them with section-specific roles: meaning, learning, capability and conviction. | Hero |
| "Knowledge becomes understanding" appeared as a broad progression in multiple areas. | Removed the full progression from Missing Layer and left the capability progression in the Capability Journey. | Capability Journey |
| "Artificial Intelligence alone is not enough" appeared in Company and New Organization language. | Removed the direct phrase and replaced it with capability and decision-quality language. | Company |
| "Self-understanding" appeared as explanatory theory language outside the core theory context. | Removed from the New Organization bottom statement and replaced with capability visibility. | New Kind of Organization |
| "The missing layer is not more software. It is Organizational Intelligence." combined two ideas. | Simplified to "The missing layer is understanding." | Missing Layer |
| "aiio turns hidden knowledge into Organizational Intelligence" over-explained the Hero. | Replaced with a simpler change statement: knowledge can become understanding. | Hero |
| The full hierarchy from knowledge to Self-Enabling Organization appeared outside the Capability Journey. | Replaced the Missing Layer list with a shorter section-specific sequence: fragmented knowledge, connected context, shared meaning. | Capability Journey |
| Thinking repeated the Hero-level category claim. | Reframed Thinking as authority: why the shift is inevitable. | Thinking |
| Company repeated the whole category story. | Reframed Company around conviction and durable capability. | Company |
| Partners repeated general Organizational Intelligence development language. | Reframed Partners around the new partner opportunity. | Partners |

## Result

The homepage and primary pages now use the same strategic ideas with clearer ownership. The Sprint did not remove the core story. It reduced repeated explanation.
