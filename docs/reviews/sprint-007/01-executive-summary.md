# 01 - Executive Summary

## Purpose

This package translates Sprint 007 into an executive review format. It summarizes the current website narrative, section roles, redundancy risks, Brand Canon usage, page priorities and recommended next roadmap.

## Executive Readout

The aiio website has moved beyond a product website and now operates as a category narrative for Organizational Intelligence. The strongest parts are the Hero, the Capability Journey, the OIS architecture moment and the final company purpose. Together they create a credible premium direction.

The main remaining risk is repetition. Several sections return to the same ideas: organizations understanding themselves, knowledge becoming understanding, capability creation and the future of self-enabling organizations. These ideas are correct, but they need sharper ownership by section.

The website also has two layers of maturity. The new premium website architecture is emerging through Home, Thinking, Platform, Partners, Company and Contact. At the same time, many legacy routes still exist and do not yet match the new Brand Canon or category narrative.

## Strategic Assessment

| Area | Status | Executive Interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Narrative | Strong | The category idea is clear and differentiated. |
| Section roles | Mostly clear | Some sections still overlap conceptually. |
| Brand Canon | Promising | BC-001 and BC-002 are central; BC-003 and BC-004 need clearer strategic assignment. |
| Page architecture | Established | Primary navigation is coherent. Legacy routes need governance. |
| Conversion path | Good foundation | Contact and demo paths exist but need more intentional journey logic. |
| Launch readiness | Not final | Strong enough for strategic review, not yet final brand launch. |

## Highest Priority Findings

1. Reduce repeated explanations of organizational self-understanding.
2. Assign one clear narrative job to each homepage section.
3. Decide which legacy routes remain part of the new aiio website.
4. Define exact Brand Canon asset usage rules for every page type.
5. Strengthen the Platform page so it explains capabilities without becoming product-heavy.

## Recommended Executive Decision

Proceed with Sprint 008 as a reduction and role-clarity sprint before adding new content, new pages or new visuals.
