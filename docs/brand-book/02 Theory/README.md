## Product Narrative Mapping

ProcessCollector creates a trusted human-readable representation of the organization.

ProcessMagnet continuously interprets organizational reality and creates Organizational Self-Understanding.

ProcessForge transforms Organizational Intelligence into reusable Organizational Capabilities.

DataForge validates and evolves capabilities through operational reality, goals, KPIs and scenarios.

## Design Principle

Products do not create business outcomes directly.

Products create and evolve organizational capabilities.

Capabilities enable Organizational Self-Empowerment.

Organizational Self-Empowerment creates Organizational Resilience.
