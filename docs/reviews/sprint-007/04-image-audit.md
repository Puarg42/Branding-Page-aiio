# 04 - Image Audit

## Purpose

Review all visible imagery against the aiio Brand Canon and identify placeholders, inconsistencies and opportunities for simplification.

## Brand Canon Criteria

- Transparency is the primary design element
- Light represents understanding
- Material feels engineered and optical
- Capability colors are used intentionally
- No sci-fi, cyberpunk, generic AI or dashboard-heavy imagery
- Every image communicates one idea
- Imagery supports the narrative rather than decorating it

## Review Questions

- Does the image feel like aiio?
- Does it explain Organizational Intelligence rather than software?
- Is the visual too literal?
- Is the visual too decorative?
- Does it compete with typography?
- Does it use the correct capability color logic?
