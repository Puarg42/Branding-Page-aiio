# 06 - Roadmap V2

## Purpose

Recommend Sprint 008-012 based on the Sprint 007 audit. This is a roadmap only. No implementation is included.

## Roadmap Principle

Do not add more story until the current story has stronger ownership, clearer page roles and consistent Brand Canon governance.

## Sprint 008 - Narrative Reduction and Section Ownership

### Goal

Reduce repetition and assign one clear narrative job to each homepage section.

### Recommended Work

- Consolidate repeated self-understanding statements.
- Make the Capability Journey the only full capability progression.
- Keep Why Now as the single investor-grade timing argument.
- Shorten Company to a final belief statement.

### Output

- Cleaner homepage narrative
- Updated section ownership map
- Reduced copy without losing meaning

## Sprint 009 - Brand Canon Governance

### Goal

Define exact rules for BC-001 through BC-004 across Home, Platform, Thinking and future pages.

### Recommended Work

- Create page-level asset rules.
- Define forbidden use cases.
- Decide whether BC-003 becomes material study, transition visual or card detail.
- Define how product pages may use cropped Brand Canon details.

### Output

- Brand Canon usage guide
- Visual system checklist
- Future image replacement plan

## Sprint 010 - Platform Page Refinement

### Goal

Make Platform explain capabilities without becoming a product portfolio.

### Recommended Work

- Lead with capability architecture.
- Keep products secondary as proof.
- Clarify Capture, Build Understanding, Enable Action and Evolve Organizations.
- Reduce product-domain language.

### Output

- Premium Platform page role
- Stronger journey from Home to Platform

## Sprint 011 - Thinking Page Authority

### Goal

Make Thinking the intellectual home of Organizational Intelligence.

### Recommended Work

- Structure theory, essays, whitepapers and research without overfilling the page.
- Connect to the theory documents.
- Establish a clear editorial hierarchy.
- Use BC-001 and BC-004 with discipline.

### Output

- Stronger thought-leadership page
- Clear bridge from brand website to research corpus

## Sprint 012 - Legacy Route Governance

### Goal

Decide what happens to every legacy route.

### Recommended Work

- Classify routes as retain, redesign, hide, redirect or archive.
- Remove legacy routes from the perceived premium journey.
- Align conversion routes with Contact.
- Keep legal routes untouched.

### Output

- Route governance decision log
- Public site map for launch
- Redirect or archive plan

## Recommended Order

1. Sprint 008 - Narrative first
2. Sprint 009 - Visual governance
3. Sprint 010 - Platform
4. Sprint 011 - Thinking
5. Sprint 012 - Legacy route decisions

## Executive Recommendation

Do not start detailed product pages before Sprint 012 is complete. The brand website should first become coherent, then the product ecosystem can be expanded from a stable foundation.
