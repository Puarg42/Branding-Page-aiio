# 02 - Subheadline Audit

## Purpose

Reduce explanatory copy so each subheadline supports the headline without competing with it.

| Location | Current | Recommendation | Decision |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Home Hero | Organizations already know more than they can use. For the first time, that knowledge can become understanding. | Strong, short and curiosity-building. | KEEP |
| Capability Journey | Knowledge becomes understanding. Understanding enables action. Action enables continuous evolution. | Strong progression, but should align with the rewritten headline. | KEEP |
| Platform Intro | aiio turns the capability journey into an operating system for organizational knowledge, understanding, action and evolution. | Too explanatory and aiio-first. | REWRITE to "The platform connects knowledge, understanding, action and evolution in one continuous system." |
| Thinking Intro | Organizational Intelligence is a structural shift in how organizations preserve knowledge, form judgment and build capability. | Strong and concise. | KEEP |
| Thinking Core Thesis Body | The theory describes why future organizations will need a continuous foundation for memory, context, judgment and capability. | Good support, but can lose words. | REWRITE to "The theory explains why future organizations need a continuous foundation for memory, context, judgment and capability." |
| Partners Intro | Organizational Intelligence creates a new partner opportunity: helping customers build durable capability instead of isolated project outcomes. | Good content, slightly long. | REWRITE to "Partners help turn Organizational Intelligence into lasting customer capability." |
| Company Intro | aiio exists because organizations need a better way to preserve what they know and turn it into durable capability. | Strong and concise. | KEEP |
| Company Mission Body | Our mission is to make organizational understanding durable, usable and capable of improving how organizations act. The software matters because the capability matters. | Good, but can be calmer and shorter. | REWRITE to "Our mission is to make organizational understanding durable and usable. The software matters because the capability matters." |
| Contact Intro | A simple entry point for demos, platform conversations and partnership discussions. | Functional but generic. | REWRITE to "For demos, platform questions and partnership conversations." |
| Contact Panel | For demos, partnerships and strategic conversations. | Repeats the intro. | REWRITE to "Tell us what you want to understand, build or evaluate." |
