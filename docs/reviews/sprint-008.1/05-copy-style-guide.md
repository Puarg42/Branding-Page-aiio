# 05 - Copy Style Guide

## Purpose

Capture the executive copy rules applied in Sprint 008.1.

## Headline Rules

- One headline, one idea.
- Prefer quotable statements over descriptive labels.
- Use category language before product language.
- Avoid acronyms in first-touch CTAs.
- Keep headlines understandable in under five seconds.

## Subheadline Rules

- Support the headline; do not explain the whole page.
- Maximum two to three short sentences.
- Remove repeated category explanations.
- Prefer concrete executive meaning over technical specificity.

## CTA Rules

- Tell the visitor what happens next.
- Prefer "See", "Read", "Request" and "Start" over generic exploration language.
- Avoid "Learn more", "Discover" and vague product curiosity.

## Tone

- Calm.
- Confident.
- Precise.
- Executive.
- No hype.
- No over-promising.
