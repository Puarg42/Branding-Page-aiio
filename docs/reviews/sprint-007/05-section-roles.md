# 05 - Section Roles

## Purpose

Define the job of every homepage and website section so future edits do not blur responsibilities.

## Section Role Principles

- Every section has one purpose
- Every section answers one question
- No section tries to explain the entire company
- Products appear only after the category and capability story are clear

## Homepage Section Roles

| Section | Role | Should Do | Should Not Do |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hero | Category opening | Create desire | Explain products |
| Problem | Tension | Name the organizational problem | Present implementation |
| Missing Layer | Breakthrough | Introduce understanding | Over-explain technology |
| A New Kind of Organization | Future state | Show what changes | Add product language |
| Imagine | Emotional bridge | Make the future tangible | Add diagrams |
| Organization Mirror | Transformation | Show fragmented knowledge becoming understanding | Become a flowchart |
| OIS | Architecture | Explain the foundation | Become a product grid |
| Why Now | Timing | Make the market argument | Repeat the full theory |
| Capabilities | Translation | Show what becomes possible | Lead with product names |
| Company | Purpose | Explain why aiio exists | Become an About Us page |
| Footer | Closing | Leave one thought | Add clutter |

## Website Page Roles

| Page | Role | Primary Question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Home | Category launch | Why Organizational Intelligence? |
| Thinking | Thought leadership | Why do we believe this? |
| Platform | System explanation | How does it work? |
| Partners | Ecosystem | Why should we build together? |

| Contact | Conversion | How do I start? |
