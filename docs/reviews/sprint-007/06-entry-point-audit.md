# 06 - Entry Point Audit

## Purpose

Review whether different visitor types enter and move through the website without confusion.

## Visitor Journeys

| Audience | Entry Page | Second Page | Third Page | Primary CTA | Expected Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| CEO | Home | Platform | Contact | Request Demo | Understand business value |
| Investor | Home | Thinking | Company | Contact | Understand category potential |
| Partner | Partners | Platform | Contact | Partner conversation | Understand ecosystem opportunity |
| Customer | Home | Platform | Contact | Request Demo | Understand practical next step |

## Entry Point Checks

- Is the next step obvious?
- Does the visitor encounter product detail too early?
- Does each page answer one question?
- Does navigation support the journey?
- Is there a clear conversion path?
