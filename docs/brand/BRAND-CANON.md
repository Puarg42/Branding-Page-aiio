# Brand Canon

## Purpose

The aiio Brand Canon defines the immutable visual assets of the Organizational Intelligence visual language.

From this point forward, illustrations are not design proposals.

They are official brand assets.

No reinterpretation is allowed.

No regeneration is allowed.

Every future illustration, animation, presentation and website component must derive from these assets.

## Brand Assets

Organizational Understanding

Brand illustration.

- Keynotes

Visual vocabulary.

Every future illustration must be composed from these primitives.

## Immutable Principles

1. Transparency over Decoration

2. Understanding over Technology

3. Calm over Spectacle

4. One Illustration. One Idea.

5. Every Illustration belongs to one visual language.

6. Material consistency is mandatory.

7. Light represents Understanding. Never Energy.

8. Capability colors represent maturity. Never branding.

## Governance

The Brand Canon has priority over individual page implementation.

Pages do not own illustrations.

Illustrations belong to the Brand Canon.

All website pages must consume Canon assets through `BrandIllustration`.
