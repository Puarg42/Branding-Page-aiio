# 01 - Narrative Map

## Purpose

Map the complete website narrative so every section can be evaluated against the intended aiio story.

## Narrative Principle

The website should move from vision to understanding to capability without feeling like a traditional software presentation.

## Homepage Sequence

| Order | Section | Narrative Role | Primary Question |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Hero | Open the category | Why should I care? |
| 2 | Problem | Name the organizational tension | What is broken today? |
| 3 | Missing Layer | Introduce the breakthrough | What is missing? |
| 4 | New Organization | Establish the future state | What becomes possible? |
| 5 | Imagine | Create emotional resonance | What would this feel like? |
| 6 | Organization Mirror | Show transformation | How does scattered knowledge become understanding? |
| 7 | OIS | Define the architecture | What is the foundation? |
| 8 | Why Now | Explain timing | Why is this possible now? |
| 9 | Capabilities | Translate vision into capability | What can organizations do? |
| 10 | Company | Explain purpose | Why does aiio exist? |
| 11 | Footer | Close emotionally | What should remain? |

## Narrative Checks

- Does each section introduce one new thought?
- Does any section repeat a previous idea without adding value?
- Does the transition between sections feel natural?
- Does the page introduce products too early?
- Does the ending feel intentional?
