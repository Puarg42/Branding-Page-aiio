# 03 - Redundancy Matrix

## Purpose

Identify repeated ideas and headline-level overlaps that should be consolidated before the next major implementation sprint.

| Statement | Appears in | Severity | Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Organizations need to understand themselves. | Hero, Missing Layer, New Kind of Organization, Company, Footer | HIGH | Keep as the central theme, but make each occurrence serve a different role: promise, diagnosis, future state, purpose, closing. |
| The future belongs to organizations that understand themselves. | Hero, Footer closing | MEDIUM | This can remain as intentional framing if the footer feels like a deliberate echo, not repetition. |
| Knowledge becomes understanding. | Missing Layer progression, Capability Journey, Thinking page, Organization Mirror | HIGH | Keep the conceptual progression in one primary section and make other occurrences lighter or more visual. |
| Understanding creates capability. | Capability Journey, New Kind of Organization, Company principles | MEDIUM | Assign the strongest articulation to Capability Journey; reduce explanatory restatement elsewhere. |
| Self-Enabling Organization is the future state. | New Kind of Organization, Imagine, theoretical progression | MEDIUM | Keep the emotional reveal in Imagine; reduce additional explanatory usage on the homepage. |
| AI alone is not enough. | New Kind of Organization, Company, Thinking | MEDIUM | Keep in Company as a belief; avoid repeating it as a general argument in multiple sections. |
| OIS is infrastructure, not another application. | OIS architecture, Platform page | LOW | Keep on homepage as a category distinction; make Platform more capability-led. |
| Every technological revolution required new infrastructure. | Why Now, Category Evolution materials | HIGH | Keep only one investor-grade Why Now section on the live page. |
| Capability journey: Capture, Understand, Enable, Evolve. | Homepage Capability Journey, Platform page | MEDIUM | Keep homepage as the emotional model; use Platform as implementation explanation. |
| Living Organizational Memory as the center of understanding. | Organization Mirror, Missing Layer, Thinking | MEDIUM | Use Organization Mirror as the visual home; avoid dense definition elsewhere. |
| The company exists to enable organizational transformation. | Company section, Contact framing, Footer CTA | LOW | Accept if Company remains reflective and Contact remains action-oriented. |
| "Platform" as both architecture and implementation. | Navigation, OIS section, Platform page | MEDIUM | Define Platform page as "how it works"; keep OIS section as "what it is." |
| Product names as supporting proof. | Capability Journey, Platform cards, legacy product pages | MEDIUM | Keep products secondary on the new site; reserve detailed product narrative for product domains. |
| Research / theory as category authority. | Thinking page, Foundation docs, homepage concepts | LOW | Accept; ensure Thinking owns the detailed explanation. |

## Executive Conclusion

The repetition is not conceptual confusion. It is mostly a maturity issue caused by successful ideas appearing in too many places. The next sprint should not add content. It should assign stronger ownership to each idea.
