# 09 - Section Ownership

## Purpose

Define the intended narrative job of each section after Sprint 008.

| Section | Role | Visitor question | Primary idea | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hero | Open the category | What has changed? | The future belongs to organizations that understand themselves. | Explore OIS / See it in action |
| Capability Journey | Explain creation | How is Organizational Intelligence created? | Knowledge becomes understanding; understanding enables action; action enables continuous evolution. | None in section |
| Platform | Explain the system | How does aiio make the journey operational? | aiio connects knowledge, people, systems and AI into one operating context. | Explore platform paths |
| Problem | Name the tension | What is broken today? | Knowledge, context, complexity and decisions are fragmenting. | None |
| Missing Layer | Introduce the breakthrough | What is missing? | Understanding is the missing layer between information and meaning. | None |
| A New Kind of Organization | Describe the future state | What kind of organization becomes possible? | Organizations that learn faster and turn learning into capability lead. | None |
| Imagine | Create emotional resonance | What does the future feel like? | Knowledge and context remain available when they are needed. | None |
| CEO Moment | Make business value tangible | What changes on Monday morning? | Critical knowledge stays usable when moments of change arrive. | None |
| Organization Mirror | Show transformation | How does scattered knowledge become visible? | Fragmented knowledge becomes relationships. | None |
| Why Now | Explain timing | Why now? | Every revolution creates new infrastructure; Organizational Intelligence is the next one. | None |
| Company | Explain conviction | Why are we building this? | aiio exists to make understanding a durable organizational capability. | None |
| Footer | Start action | How do I start? | Move from interest to conversation. | Request a Demo / Explore the Platform |

## Ownership Rule

If a future edit needs to explain the full capability progression, it belongs in the Capability Journey or Platform page. Other sections should reference only the part of the idea they own.
